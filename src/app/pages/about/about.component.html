About Me

Hello, my name is Caleb Hurshman! I am a junior software developer originally from Ypsilanti, Michigan. I got started programming by doing free, online coding courses when I was in middle school. Through middle and high school I started attending ID Tech Camps at the University of Michigan, which were week-long programming crash courses. My senior year, I finally took a formal course, after which I decided to major in computer science. After three and a half years at Calvin University I graduated with a BS in Computer Science. For a list of relevant courses, visit the Education tab on this page. When I'm not coding I enjoy spending time with friends and family, weightlifting, rock climbing, gaming, cooking, and enjoying nature.

In my time as a student I worked for the Computer Science department as a teacher's assisstant, lab monitor, and grader for multiple semesters. Over the summer of 2022, I interned at Maestro in Kalamazoo, Michigan. For a seventeen week period I was fully immersed in an agile team environment and was given the opportunity to make meaningful contributions both to the core product and to the team itself. I worked full stack developing software that was used internally, as well as a full-fledged event calendar feature that was deployed for customer use.

I graduated in December 2022, and I am currently looking for a full-time role as a software engineer. My ideal company is product-driven, with an emphasis on culture, work-life balance, learning, and growth opportunities. I specialize in TypeScript/JavaScript, Python, HTML, SCSS/CSS, and Angular, but I have exposure to many languages and technologies. I've worked in languages like Java, C#, C/C++, Ruby, Swift, Clojure, and Ada. Alongside Angular I've worked with frameworks like React Native and .NET. I have experience both with relational databases in PostgreSQL/ElephantSQL and in NoSQL database systems like Google Firestore. I bring a passion for learning and growth that drives me as a professional and I take pride in my work. If any of my experiences or values resonate with you, feel free to reach out to me on LinkedIn or by email. Thanks!
